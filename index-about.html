About Me

How I Became a UX Designer

I was double majoring in Digital Content Department during my college, and that was when I started to hear about UI&UX and fell in love with it. I love the progress of understanding users and really helping them by developing something cool and useful.

How I Manage My Team

My motto is: "Always make communication more effective". Seldom did I encounter arguments with my teammates, and if there were different opinions, I would always try my best to understand each point of view, and then conclude a solution that at least everyone agreed on.

I love working with a group of people, making me more motivated on creating cool stuff.

How I Spend My Free Time

I use my spare time to study crypto investment, which is part of my passive income. I am also managing a second-hand shopping store online.

On weekends, I would hang out with friends, go hiking, or just quietly enjoy my leisure at home. Everything seems interesting to me, and I learn fast, so basically I am always willing to try new things.
